  <h1>Uni Media</h1>

  <h2>1. Project Title</h2>
  <p><strong>Uni Media</strong></p>

  <h2>2. Introduction</h2>
  <p>
    Uni Media is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to enhance the communication, collaboration, and academic experience for university students and administrators. It consists of two primary modules: a Student Portal and a Teacher Portal. The platform allows students to stay connected with their university, manage their academic activities, and engage with their peers. University administrators have efficient tools to manage student information, communication, and other key administrative tasks.
  </p>

  <h2>3. Purpose of Project</h2>
  <p>The Uni Media project aims to create a centralized platform for university students and administrators to enhance communication, academic engagement, and campus life management. The key objectives include:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Streamlining University Communication:</strong> Centralizes updates, news, and notices for students.</li>
    <li><strong>Simplifying Administrative Tasks:</strong> Helps admins manage student/department info, share notices, upload results, and track group activity.</li>
    <li><strong>Enhancing Student Experience:</strong> Provides tools for result checking, bus schedules, and real-time messaging.</li>
    <li><strong>Encouraging Community Building:</strong> Supports student interest groups, peer interaction, and participation in campus life.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>4. Project Features</h2>

  <h3>I. Student Portal</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>User Registration & Login:</strong> Register with Name, Department, Student ID, Email, Password. Login with Student ID and Password.</li>
    <li><strong>User Profile:</strong> Includes profile picture and bio, editable by students.</li>
    <li><strong>Post Updates:</strong> Share photos, videos, statuses; like and comment on posts.</li>
    <li><strong>Department Section:</strong> View department-specific news and faculty info.</li>
    <li><strong>Notice Board:</strong> Access official university notices.</li>
    <li><strong>Group Management:</strong> Join admin-created groups, share media.</li>
    <li><strong>Result Section:</strong> Check results by Student ID.</li>
    <li><strong>Bus Schedule:</strong> View transport schedule.</li>
    <li><strong>Logout Option:</strong> Secure session logout.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>II. Admin Portal</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>User Management:</strong> Manage faculty and student accounts.</li>
    <li><strong>Department Management:</strong> Create and update departments and batches.</li>
    <li><strong>Notice Management:</strong> Post notices for students.</li>
    <li><strong>Group Management:</strong> Create/manage groups, moderate posts.</li>
    <li><strong>Result Management:</strong> Upload results tied to Student IDs.</li>
    <li><strong>Bus Schedule Management:</strong> Upload/edit bus schedules.</li>
    <li><strong>Logout Option:</strong> Secure session logout.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>7. Conclusion</h2>
  <p>
    Uni Media represents a vital step toward modernizing university communication and student services, helping to create a more dynamic, interactive, and organized university experience. Its successful implementation empowers both students and administrators, making campus life more efficient, engaging, and connected.
  </p>
